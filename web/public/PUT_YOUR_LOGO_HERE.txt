Drop your logo and favicon files into this folder:

  logo.png     ← School logo shown in navbar + login page
                 Recommended: PNG, transparent background, ~200×60px or square
                 Will auto-scale to fit

  favicon.png  ← Browser tab icon (shown in tab + bookmarks)
                 Recommended: PNG, 64×64px or 256×256px, square

Both files are served at:
  http://localhost:4000/logo.png
  http://localhost:4000/favicon.png

After adding the files, just rebuild:
  docker compose -f docker-compose.sqlite.yml up -d --build
